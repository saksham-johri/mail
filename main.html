Greetings from Saturangle,

This time Saturangle Club of English Department is back with an Online Blog Writing Competition.

A Blog is an online journal or informational website displaying information, with latest posts appearing first. It is a platform where a writer or even a group of writers share their views.

The Competition Will Be Conducted Online!

Topics for Competition are:

  • Technology that empowers me
  • How to make best use of time
  • Jobs vs Passion

General rules to be followed during writing are:

  • Word limit is 200 words only.
  • If plagiarism will be discovered in participants’ writeup , they will be straight away disqualified.
  • The duration for submitting the writeup is from 13th October, 2018 to 20th October, 2018.
  • No writeup will be entertained after 20th October, 2018.
  • Participants will write their blogs as text files and mail it to saturangle@gla.ac.in.

Only one winner will be selected from each topic and will be rewarded.

NOTE:- Certificates will be provided to all the participants.
